Controls

  • Press Left & Right arrowkeys to control player movement
  • Press Up. Hold CTRL at the same time to perform a super jump
  • Slide on rails to gain points
  • Rails that are close to the top are worth extra points
  • Points multiplier gets reset when the player touches the ground
  • Going backwards will cost points

Press TAB to submit score
